Welcome to All Tools Portal (the "Site"). We are a directory of online tools and resources. This privacy policy explains how we handle information.

We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of these external websites before using their services or providing any personal information. We do not assume any responsibility or liability for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

Information Collection and Use (Our Site)

All Tools Portal is a static website designed to provide information and links. We do not require user registration, and we do not use cookies to track your activity on our site for our own direct analytical purposes. We do not directly collect personal data such as names, email addresses, or IP addresses for creating user profiles on our Site.

The email links provided in the footer (e.g., for "Help" or "Contact") are standard mailto: links that will use your own email client to send an email. We do not process or store these communications through our website itself.

Log Data from Hosting Provider

Like most websites, our web hosting provider may collect standard Log Data that your browser sends whenever you visit our Site. This Log Data may include information such as your computer's Internet Protocol ("IP") address (which may be anonymized or generalized by the provider), browser type, browser version, the pages of our Site that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, and other general, non-personally identifiable statistics. This data is typically used by the hosting provider for website analytics (e.g., traffic measurement, site performance) and security purposes. This data is not directly used by All Tools Portal to identify individual users.

Embedded Content and Third-Party Services

Some content on our site (like fonts from Google Fonts, icons from Font Awesome, or JavaScript libraries from CDNs like Tailwind CSS) may be served from third-party Content Delivery Networks (CDNs). These services may collect data as per their own privacy policies. We encourage you to be aware of this when interacting with such content.

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under age 13 without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers if any such data were inadvertently collected by our hosting provider in a way accessible to us.
